Frequently Asked Questions

What equipment do I need for the courses?

Equipment requirements vary by course. Beginner courses can be completed with any digital camera, including smartphones. Intermediate and advanced courses typically require a DSLR or mirrorless camera with manual controls. Specific requirements are listed on each course page.

How do the online courses work?

Our online courses include video lessons, downloadable resources, assignments, and feedback. Most courses also include live Q&A sessions with instructors. You'll have lifetime access to course materials and can learn at your own pace.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes, we offer interest-free payment plans for courses over $200. You can spread the cost over 3, 6, or 12 months, depending on the course. Details are available on the enrollment page for each course.

Is there a refund policy?

We offer a 14-day satisfaction guarantee for all our courses. If you're not satisfied, you can request a full refund within 14 days of purchase, no questions asked.
