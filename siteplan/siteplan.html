Site Plan: Digital Photography Academy

Site Name

Digital Photography Academy

This name was chosen because it clearly communicates our educational focus on digital photography while maintaining a professional and authoritative tone. The word "Academy" suggests a structured learning environment and professional development.

Domain: digital-photo-academy.com

Site Purpose

The Digital Photography Academy website will provide:

  • Structured online photography courses for all skill levels
  • Equipment reviews and recommendations
  • Student work gallery with peer feedback system
  • Professional photographer mentoring sessions
  • Photography business development resources

Scenarios

Scenario 1

"As a beginner photographer who just bought their first DSLR camera, what courses should I take to learn the fundamentals of photography and how to properly use my camera?"

Scenario 2

"I'm an intermediate photographer looking to start a photography business. Where can I find resources about pricing my services and building a client base?"
